Chameleon Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

Chameleon ("we", "our", "the app") is a privacy-first browser and digital identity manager. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. Chameleon is designed with privacy at its core:

  • No analytics or tracking: We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking frameworks.
  • No server-side storage: All your data (vault items, browsing history, identities, preferences) is stored locally on your device only.
  • No account required: You do not need to create an account to use Chameleon.

2. Vault Data

Passwords, credit cards, and secure notes stored in the Chameleon Vault are encrypted using AES-256 encryption and stored in the iOS Keychain on your device. We never have access to your vault data or master password. If you forget your master password, we cannot recover it.

3. Browsing Data

Chameleon Browser uses non-persistent (ephemeral) web data storage. Browsing history and tab data are stored locally in memory and are not transmitted to any server. DNS-over-HTTPS queries are sent to public DNS providers (Cloudflare, Google, Quad9) to encrypt your DNS lookups — these providers have their own privacy policies.

4. Subscriptions

Subscription purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information. Subscription status is verified locally using StoreKit.

5. Third-Party Services

Chameleon does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs that collect user data. The only external network requests are:

  • DNS-over-HTTPS queries to public DNS providers (when the Chameleon Protocol is active)
  • Standard HTTPS web requests initiated by you in the browser
  • Apple's StoreKit for subscription management

6. Children's Privacy

Chameleon is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.
